About

Welcome to FlareLint 1.2!

You can use FlareLint to make production of your MadCap Flare output faster and less tedious. Use it to produce more consistent, higher-quality content for your end users.

FlareLint scans your MadCap Flare project for conformance to your rules then generates a report. For example, FlareLint reports when a topic does not start with an h1 element.

FlareLint is not suited for catching language and visual errors including spelling, grammar, layout, and so on. Writers are better equipped to find and solve these problems.

FlareLint works independently of Flare. It inspects source files in Flare projects directly and does not rely on Flare's output. FlareLint does not modify your Flare project or its source files. You don't have to open a project in Flare or build a Flare target before using FlareLint.
