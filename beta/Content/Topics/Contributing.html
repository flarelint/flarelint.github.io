Sending Feedback

FlareLint is so useful to us that we want other MadCap Flare users to benefit from it. We appreciate and welcome your feedback and improvements. Share with us and other FlareLint users!

Does FlareLint save you time or help you ship better documentation? Do you have ideas to improve FlareLint or have you made changes? Send encouragement, bug reports, and improvements to www.freelists.org/list/flarelint.
