Defining a Standard Set of Rules

You can specify a set of rules that your team uses. By standardizing the rules that each member of your team uses, you can ensure that your Flare projects are consistent.

Since FlareLint rules are simply plain text files, there are a number of ways that you can manage these files for your team. This topic describes one of these methods, but you can use other methods that are more convenient.

To define a standard set of rules:

  1. Create a shared folder named FlareLint that all members of your team may access.

  2. In this shared folder, copy your teamâ€™s rules.

  3. Each time you add or remove rules in the shared folder, ask each member of your team to copy and replace the contents of their existing personal shared rules folder, %APPDATA%\FlareLint, with the contents of the shared FlareLint folder.
